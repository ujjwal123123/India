India
Size and Location

India is one of the ancient civilsations in the world. It has achieved multi-faceted socio-economic progress during the last five decades. It has moved forward displaying remarkable progress in the field of agriculture, industry, technology and overall economic development. India has also contributed significantly to the making of world history.

Size

The land mass of India has an area of 3.28 million square km. India's total area accounts for about 2.4 per cent of the total geopraphical area of the world. India is the seventh largest country of the world. India has a land boundry of about 15,200 km and the total length of the coast line of the mainland including Andamam and Nicobar and Lakshadweep is 7,516.6 km.

Location

India is a vast country. Lying entirely in thes Northern hemisphere the main land extends between latitude 8° 6′ N and longitudes 68° 7′ E and 97° 25′ E.

Neighbours

India has 29 states and 7 UT. India shares its land boundries with Pakistan and Afghanistan in northwest, China (Tibet), Nepal and Bhutan in the north and Myanmar and Bangladesh in the east. Our southern neighbour across the sea consist of the two Island countries, namely Sri lanka and Maldives.
