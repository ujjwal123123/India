Neighbours

India occurpies an important strategic position in South Asia. India has 29 states and 7 UT. India shares its land boundries with Pakistan and Afghanistan in northwest, China (Tibet), Nepal and Bhutan in the north and Myanmar and Bangladesh in the east. Our southern neighbour across the sea consist of the two Island countries, namely Sri lanka and Maldives. Sri Lanka is separated from Idnia by a narrow channel of sea formed by the Palk Strait and the Gulf of Mannar while Maldives Islands are situated to the south of the Lakshadweep Islands.

India has had strong geographical and historical links with her neighbour.
